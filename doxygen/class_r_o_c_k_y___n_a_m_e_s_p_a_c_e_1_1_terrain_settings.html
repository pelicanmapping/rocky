◆ skirtRatio [1/2]

optional<float> ROCKY_NAMESPACE::TerrainSettings::skirtRatio = 0.0f

Ratio of skirt height to tile width. The "skirt" is geometry extending down from the edge of terrain tiles meant to hide cracks between adjacent levels of detail. A value of 0 means no skirt.
